Two-dimensional correlation analysis of NMR and MALDI mass spectrometry data to identify co-varying lipid and metabolite features in tumor cell models. Reveals spectral interactions that serve as candidate biomarkers for metabolic phenotyping in cancer diagnostics.

## Contributions

1. Developed cross-modal correlation framework pairing NMR chemical shift assignments with MALDI spatial features.
2. Applied statistical filtering to isolate cancer-state-dependent spectral co-variance.
3. Presented findings at JHU School of Medicine Radiology Research Day (Pre-Clinical Poster Award).
